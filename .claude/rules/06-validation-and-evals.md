# Rule: validation and evaluation

## When touching trust-critical areas
Add or update validation for:
- ingestion correctness,
- chunk quality,
- retrieval relevance,
- citation support,
- abstention behavior,
- failure visibility.

## Minimum expectation
- Do not treat answer fluency as proof of correctness.
- Prefer retrieval- and evidence-level checks for RAG work.
- When no automated validation exists yet, leave clear TODOs tied to the relevant evaluation doc sections.
